[1] [vocabulary] [easy] "mine" 的中文是？
   ✓ A. 我的
     B. 你的
     C. 他的
     D. 她的
   解析: mine = 我的，名词性物主代词。

[2] [vocabulary] [easy] "yours" 的中文是？
   ✓ A. 你的
     B. 我的
     C. 他的
     D. 她的
   解析: yours = 你的/你们的。

[3] [vocabulary] [easy] "tidy" 的中文是？
   ✓ A. 整洁的
     B. 凌乱的
     C. 安静的
     D. 大声的
   解析: tidy = 整洁的，收拾整齐。

[4] [vocabulary] [easy] "messy" 的中文是？
   ✓ A. 凌乱的
     B. 整洁的
     C. 干净的
     D. 明亮的
   解析: messy = 凌乱的，不整洁。

[5] [vocabulary] [medium] "他的" 用英语怎么说（名词性）？
   ✓ A. his
     B. him
     C. he
     D. her
   解析: his = 他的（名词性物主代词，his既是形容词性也是名词性）。

[6] [vocabulary] [medium] "她的" 用英语怎么说（名词性）？
   ✓ A. hers
     B. her
     C. she
     D. herself
   解析: hers = 她的，如 This book is hers.

[7] [vocabulary] [medium] "收拾好" 用英语怎么说？
   ✓ A. put away
     B. take away
     C. throw away
     D. run away
   解析: put away = 收拾好，放回原处。

[8] [vocabulary] [medium] "谁的" 用英语怎么说？
   ✓ A. whose
     B. who
     C. whom
     D. who's
   解析: whose = 谁的，用于提问物品归属。

[9] [vocabulary] [hard] 补全单词: "我们的" 名词性 = o _ _ s
   ✓ A. ours
     B. our
     C. ourselves
     D. ourself
   解析: ours = 我们的，如 This classroom is ours.

[10] [vocabulary] [hard] "他们的" 名词性物主代词是？
   ✓ A. theirs
     B. their
     C. them
     D. themselves
   解析: theirs = 他们的/她们的/它们的。

[11] [grammar] [easy] This book is _____. (它是我的)
   ✓ A. mine
     B. my
     C. me
     D. I
   解析: 后面没有名词，用名词性物主代词 mine。

[12] [grammar] [easy] Is this _____ pen? (你的)
   ✓ A. your
     B. yours
     C. you
     D. yourselves
   解析: 后面有名词 pen，用形容词性物主代词 your。

[13] [grammar] [easy] These are _____ shoes. (她的)
   ✓ A. her
     B. hers
     C. she
     D. herself
   解析: 后面有名词 shoes，用形容词性物主代词 her。

[14] [grammar] [medium] — Whose bag is this? — It's _____. (我的)
   ✓ A. mine
     B. my
     C. me
     D. myself
   解析: 回答"这是谁的包"时，用名词性物主代词 mine，等于 my bag。

[15] [grammar] [medium] — Is this _____ bike? — No, it's _____. (你的)(他的)
   ✓ A. your, his
     B. yours, his
     C. your, him
     D. yours, him
   解析: 第一个空后有bike填your，第二个空后无名词填his。

[16] [grammar] [medium] Put _____ your toys, please.
   ✓ A. away
     B. on
     C. off
     D. up
   解析: put away = 收拾好。

[17] [grammar] [hard] 哪一处有错误？"This is mine book. That is yours."
     A. This
   ✓ B. mine
     C. That
     D. yours
   解析: mine 是名词性，不能修饰名词。应改为 my。

[18] [grammar] [hard] 哪一处有错误？"The cat is her. The dog is mine."
     A. The cat
   ✓ B. her
     C. The dog
     D. mine
   解析: her 是形容词性，不能单独使用。应改为 hers。

[19] [speaking] [easy] 请跟读：This is my book.
   解析: 注意 this 的 /ð/ 和 book 的 /ʊ/。
   跟读: This is my book.

[20] [speaking] [easy] 请跟读：Is this your pen?
   解析: 注意一般疑问句的升调。
   跟读: Is this your pen?

[21] [speaking] [medium] 请跟读：Whose bag is this?
   解析: 注意 whose 的 /huːz/ 发音。
   跟读: Whose bag is this?

[22] [speaking] [medium] 请跟读：It's mine. Put it away.
   解析: 注意连读 "put it"。
   跟读: It's mine. Put it away.

[23] [speaking] [hard] 请跟读：This is ours, not theirs.
   解析: 注意 ours 和 theirs 的 /z/ 结尾。
   跟读: This is ours, not theirs.

[24] [speaking] [hard] 请跟读：Please tidy up your room!
   解析: 祈使句的语调要肯定有力。
   跟读: Please tidy up your room!

[25] [boss] [hard] 选择正确的句子：
   ✓ A. This is my pen. That is yours.
     B. This is mine pen. That is your.
     C. This is my. That is your.
     D. This is mine. That is your.
   解析: 第一空后有名词pen用my，第二空后无名词用yours。

[26] [boss] [hard] — _____ book is this? — It's _____ .
   ✓ A. Whose, hers
     B. Who, her
     C. Whose, her
     D. Who, hers
   解析: 提问归属用 Whose，回答用名词性物主代词 hers。

[27] [boss] [hard] 选出与 "They are our books." 意思相同的句子：
   ✓ A. These books are ours.
     B. These books are our.
     C. These are we books.
     D. These books are them.
   解析: our books = ours，物主代词转换。

[28] [boss] [hard] 请选出错误的一项并改正："Her room is tidy, but mine room is messy."
     A. Her → She
   ✓ B. mine → my
     C. tidy → tidily
     D. messy → mess
   解析: mine 后面不能跟名词 room，应改为 my room。
